07Motion Principles
01
Fast out, soft land
Everything accelerates quickly then eases to rest. Never a linear move. cubic-bezier(.16,1,.3,1)
02
Spring for the dot
The signal dot always overshoots slightly — it's alive. cubic-bezier(.34,1.56,.64,1)
03
Stagger everything
Never animate two things at once. Offset by 80–120ms. Creates rhythm, not chaos.
04
Never block intent
All animations skippable. Max 300ms for UI. Splash is the only exception (1.8s).
Ryvo Motion Identity — 7 animation systems, all live in browser.
